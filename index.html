Simple Rules

Pick one golfer each week from the PGA Tour, LIV Golf, or Champions Tour. Their tournament earnings become your score. Use each player only once per season.

Live Tracking

Watch your player climb the leaderboard in real-time. See projected earnings update as the tournament unfolds.

Challenge Friends

Create private pools, invite friends, and compete head-to-head throughout the entire golf season.

Accumulate Earnings

Your total earnings accumulate week after week. The highest total at season's end wins the pool.

How to Play

1

Create or Join a Pool

Start your own pool and invite friends, or join an existing one with an invite link. Choose from PGA Tour, LIV Golf, or Champions Tour.

2

Pick One Golfer Each Week

Before each tournament begins, select one golfer from the field. Their prize money earnings for that event count as your score. Choose wisely you can only use each golfer once all season!

3

Track Live Results

Follow your pick's progress in real-time on the live leaderboard. Watch projected earnings update throughout each round as your golfer moves up or down the field.

4

Climb the Standings

Your earnings accumulate throughout the season. The pool member with the highest total earnings at the end of the season wins. Manage your player selections carefully once you use a golfer, they're off your board for the rest of the year.
